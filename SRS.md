MVP Scope
Core MVP should include real-time cash positions across multiple accounts, basic cash flow forecasting, payment initiation with
simple  approval  workflows,  multi-currency  support,  standard  reporting,  and  integration  with  2-3  major  banks.  Focus  on
mid-market companies with 10-100 entities as initial target market.
## Competitive Landscape
Kyriba   leads   the   cloud   treasury   space   with   comprehensive   functionality,   competing   against   established   players   like
TreasuryXpress,  GTreasury,  and  traditional  on-premise  solutions.  The  market  is  consolidating  with  larger  financial  software
companies acquiring specialized treasury vendors. Opportunity exists for next-generation platforms that leverage AI, blockchain,
and modern UX design.
